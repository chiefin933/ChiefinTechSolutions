Cookie Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how ChiefinTechSolutions uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website. We believe in complete transparency and want you to understand how we optimize your experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device by your web browser when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device, remember preferences, and analyze how you interact with the pages to improve overall performance and usability.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies to keep our website running smoothly:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for you to navigate our site and use its core features, such as our persistent Dark/Light theme toggles or menu states. The website cannot function correctly without these.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies: These collect anonymous statistical information about how visitors navigate our pages (e.g., Google Analytics). We use this aggregate data solely to improve page load times, layout, and visual flow.
  • Preference Cookies: Used to remember preferences you make (such as choice of theme or form autofill cues) so you don't have to configure them on every visit.

3. How Long Do Cookies Stay?

We use both session cookies (which expire automatically when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted) to provide a seamless browsing experience.

4. Managing and Disabling Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings menu. You can block all cookies, delete existing ones, or choose to receive a notification before a cookie is stored. Please note that disabling essential cookies may impact the display or functionality of certain features on our site, such as the persistent dark mode configuration.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal compliance parameters. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
