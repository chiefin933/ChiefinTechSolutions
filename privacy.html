Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At ChiefinTechSolutions, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal or organizational data we collect from you. This Privacy Policy explains how we gather, process, and secure information when you visit our website or use our professional technology services.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to deliver our services, communicate with you, or improve your website experience:

  • Contact Details: Your name, email address, telephone number, and company name when you fill out our contact form or request a consultation.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data regarding your visit (e.g., page views, duration of visit, device type) collected via cookies to optimize website performance.
  • Project Specifications: Technical details, requirements, and business parameters you share with us to plan and develop your custom solutions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for professional business purposes, including:

  • Responding to your inquiries and providing technical consultation.
  • Designing, building, testing, and supporting custom software and infrastructure solutions.
  • Sending periodic project updates, engineering insights, or company news (you can opt out at any time).
  • Ensuring compliance, security audits, and preventing unauthorized access to our systems.

3. Data Security & Protection

We implement industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure. All project details are managed under strict confidentiality agreements (NDAs) to protect your proprietary business workflows.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, rent, or lease your personal or organizational information to third parties. We only share information with trusted service partners (such as secure cloud infrastructure providers) strictly to deploy your systems, and only with your explicit approval.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the information we hold about you, request corrections, or ask for the deletion of your contact information from our databases. To exercise these rights, please contact hello@chiefintechsolutions.co.ke.
